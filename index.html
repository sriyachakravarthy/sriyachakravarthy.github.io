about

Hello! My name is Sriya, a passionate AI researcher and developer with a Master's in Artificial Intelligence from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. My expertise lies in developing and optimizing deep learning models, with a particular focus on natural language processing and speech recognition.

During my academic journey at the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology, I honed my skills in electronics and communication engineering, which has given me a solid foundation in understanding complex systems and hardware-software integration.

Over the years, I have worked on various projects, including implementing Transformer models for translation tasks and developing innovative solutions for large-scale data processing.

I also have interest in Computational Biology, whose related work can be found in my publications section.

My core skills include Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and advanced machine learning techniques. I'm always eager to learn and explore new technologies that can push the boundaries of AI and machine learning.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing Badminton, Journaling, participating in marathons, and collaborating on open-source projects.
